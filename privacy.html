Fizzy Affairs is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website (www.fizzyaffairs.com) or contact us for bookings and inquiries.

We collect the following personal data when you contact us or make a booking:

3. How We Collect Your Data

  • Booking forms on our website.
  • Direct email communication.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and process bookings.
  • To communicate with you about our services.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your personal data is securely stored in:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data is only used for internal purposes to provide our services.

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or request the deletion of your data.

  • Lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority (CNIL) if you believe your data rights have been violated.
